Let's Expose New Music Together

This application communicates with the TasteKid API to provide the individual user a video and information that pertains to new, personalized music. Simply enter your name, the name of an artist you are fond of, and hit submit. Then, press on a link provided on a artist that intrigues you. You will be directed to some new content, on youtube, by a recommended artist!
